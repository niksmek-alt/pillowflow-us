Program rules

Clear, fair, and claim-safe.

Reward and lead rules

  • Rewards are paid after the applicable milestone is verified and any 30-day return or cancellation period has passed.
  • Duplicate leads are credited to the first accepted referrer.
  • Self-referrals, fabricated contacts, spam, impersonation, and undisclosed paid lead lists do not qualify.
  • PillowFlow may reject unqualified, unreachable, existing, or fraudulent leads and may cancel rewards for abuse.

Responsible wording

  • Do not promise medical, safety, productivity, retention, or pain-relief outcomes.
  • PillowFlow is an ergonomic thigh-support device, not a medical device.
  • Describe a pilot as an evaluation, not proof of a health or business result.
  • Only use approved, current product materials.
