Compliance note

Observational data only — not causal.

PillowFlow pilot reports summarize feedback and usage observations. They do not prove medical, ergonomic, safety, or productivity outcomes, and PF-X01 is not a medical device.

  • IncludedComfort ratings, fit notes, usage patterns, and implementation feedback.
  • Not includedClinical claims, treatment claims, or causal claims about incident reduction.
  • Buyer outputA professional pilot closeout that supports a practical go, no-go, or next-test decision.
