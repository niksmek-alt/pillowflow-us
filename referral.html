Qualified fleet lead

A useful introduction has four essentials.

The company must be real, reachable, relevant to PillowFlow, and new to our active sales process.

01 / SCALE

10+ drivers or vehicles

An active commercial, nonprofit, or institutional fleet with meaningful seated driving work.

02 / COMPANY

Verifiable operation

A legitimate organization with a working website or other identifiable business presence.

03 / CONTACT

Relevant decision-maker

A manager or leader connected to fleet operations, safety, HR, procurement, or driver programs.

04 / FIT

Reason to evaluate

A credible interest in driver comfort, pilot testing, retention, or the day-to-day seat experience.

Program rules

Clear, fair, and claim-safe.

Lead and reward rules

  • The lead must be new to PillowFlow’s active referral or sales pipeline.
  • Duplicate leads are credited to the first accepted referrer.
  • Self-referrals, fabricated contacts, purchased lists, impersonation, and spam do not qualify.
  • Paid-pilot and order rewards require verified payment and completion of applicable refund or cancellation periods.
  • PillowFlow may reject unqualified, unreachable, misleading, or fraudulent submissions.

Responsible product language

  • PillowFlow is an ergonomic thigh-support device, not a medical device.
  • Do not claim that PillowFlow cures, treats, prevents, or relieves pain or a medical condition.
  • Do not promise safety, retention, productivity, or health outcomes.
  • Describe a fleet pilot as an evaluation, not proof of a product or business result.
  • Submit only business contact information you are permitted to share.
